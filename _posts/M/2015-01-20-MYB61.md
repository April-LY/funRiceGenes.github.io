* **Key message**  
    + Multiple experiments demonstrated that transcription factors NAC29/31 and MYB61 are CESA regulators in rice; NAC29/31 directly regulates MYB61, which in turn activates CESA expression

* **Connection**  
    + __IIP4__, __MYB61__, [An uncanonical CCCH-tandem zinc finger protein represses secondary wall synthesis and controls mechanical strength in rice.](http://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed?term=An uncanonical CCCH-tandem zinc finger protein represses secondary wall synthesis and controls mechanical strength in rice.%5BTitle%5D),  Gene expression and transactivation activity assays revealed that IIP4 negatively regulates expression of MYB61 and CESAs without binding to the promoters of these genes
